How much will I pay to attend the event?
It is free to attend the OAU Fashion week events. We've planned the event with students participation as a top priority, hence, for the most part of the event week, you will be able to attend absoluetly free of charge.
How can i register as a designer and is it free?
Designers participation is not free. All designers interested in participating at the OAU Fashion Week will be able to establish their interest by paying for one of the packages which will be announced soon. To register as a designer, click on the "Showcase on the Rumway" or "Designer's Registration" on the homepage.
I am not a student of OAU, is there a way i can follow on with the event?
You can join any of the thriving communities of the event on your desired social media platform: whatsapp, twitter, instagram, or youtube.
Is there a way i can join the planning team?
Yes, you can join the train of amazing volunteers working hard to make the event a success. we are actively recruiting volunteers and we will be delighted to have you join us too.
How do i come in as a sponsor?
We welcome all sponsors who are willing to contribute to make the event a sucess. you can reach out to us by clicking the "Sponsor the Event" button on the homepage.
